# Decisions
- As part of the new unincorporate state mechanic, there is an unincorporate state decision that appears as part of the new mechanic. More details can be found in the new game mechanics section of the changelog
- A game master decision has been added to allow for one of the players (a moderator) to become the game master of the campaign. The game master will be able to give/take money from a country's treasury, give/take a country infamy, give a buff/debuff to a country's prestige/authority/influence with a base increase modifier or a multiplier modifier. More details can be found in the new game mechanics section of the changelog

# Decrees
- An unincorporate state decree has been added to allow for the unincorporation of a state. This is part of the new unincorporate state mechanic. More details can be found in the new game mechanics section of the changelog

# Defines
- The maximum number of autosaves has been increased from 5 to 12 to allow for more autosaves in multiplayer games
- The diplomatic play abandon support relations impact has been decreased from 50 to 20 to make it easier to abandon support for relations in multiplayer games
- The default war goal has been changed back to money transfer instead of humiliation
- The migration minimum state pops has been increased from 20,000 to 50,000 to increase the number of pops a state can have before they can start emigrating away in multiplayer games
- The migration state population threshold has been increased from 100,000 to 250,000 to increase the number of pops a state can have with reduced emigration in multiplayer games
- The reinvestment efficiency maximum has been decreased from 3.0 to 2.0 to reduce the effectiveness of reinvestment of small countries in multiplayer games
- The reinvestment base efficiency threshold has been decreased from 50,000,000 to 7,500,000 to reduce the effectiveness of reinvestment of small countries in multiplayer games
- The military formation weekly maximum unit upgrades for army has been decreased from 0.1 to 0.02 to reduce the rate of unit upgrades in multiplayer games, meaning it should take 50 weeks to fully upgrade an army instead of 10 weeks
- The military formation weekly maximum unit upgrades for fleet has been decreased from 0.05 to 0.01 to reduce the rate of unit upgrades in multiplayer games, meaning it should take 100 weeks to fully upgrade a fleet instead of 20 weeks
- The devastation increase rate has been decreased from 0.1 to 0.02 to make daily occupation of a state less devastating, scaling with level of occupation
- The devastation from battles has been increased from 0.1 to 1.0 to make each battle ten times more devastating to the state, so a battle that would normally cause 0.1 devastation will now cause 1.0 devastation
- The law enactment law commitment effect has been removed so players cannot use the law commitment to enact laws faster in multiplayer games

# Diplomatic Actions
- When a country joins a Sovereign Empire type power bloc, they will automatically become a subject of the power bloc leader; either as a tributary or protectorate depending on whether the country is recognized or unrecognized
- A game master diplomatic action has been added as part of the game master game mechanic. More details can be found in the new game mechanics section of the changelog
- Countries will now be able to intervene in an ongoing war that they are not a participant in, provided they meet the following conditions:
	- They are not a decentralized country
	- The war is not a revolutionary or secessionist war
	- The country is allowed to do independent diplomatic actions
	- The country is not a war with the country whose side they want to join on
	- The country can reach the country whose side they want to join on, by either of the following methods:
		- Have direct access via bordering them
		- Have access through an ally who is also at war with the country whose side they want to join on
		- Have access through a subject
		- Have access through an enemy third party
	- Have diplomatic relations with the country whose side they want to join on
	- Have cordial or higher relations with the country whose side they want to join on
- A new diplomatic action has been added to allow a country to turn a newly released subject into a personal union, provided the country is a monarchy and the subject was just released by the overlord. More information can be found in the new game mechanics section of the changelog
- Rivalries can only be formed between countries of either equal rank or against a country of a higher rank. Recognized countries can not rival unrecognized countries, and unrecognized countries can not rival recognized countries

# Diplomatic Plays
- The Humiliation war goal can only be used on countries that a country officially rivals

# Game Mechanics
- Game Master:
	- The game master can give/take money from a country's treasury, give/take a country infamy, give a buff/debuff to a country's prestige/authority/influence with a base increase modifier or a multiplier modifier. This is done by using the game master decision, and then using the game master diplomatic action on a country to perform a game master action or they can use a decision to perform a game master action on themselves
- Ideological Union Synergize Laws:
	- When a country is a non-leader member of an Ideological Union power bloc, their laws will be synergized with the laws of the power bloc leader over time if any of their laws of the following law groups is different:
		- Governance Principles
		- Distribution of Power
		- Economic System
		- Land Reform
		- Free Speech
		- Slavery
	- The synergization is tracked in a journal entry, which starts a weekly process where there is a 4% weekly chance that the law synergization for that law group will progress by one step. Each step will have a -1 governmant legitimacy penalty, which increases by 1 for each step that the law synergization progresses.
	- When the synergization reaches the final step, 12, the law will be changed in the non-leader power bloc member to match the law of the power bloc leader, and the journal entry will be completed, with a final -12 government legitimacy penalty will be applied to the country that will decay over 10 years
- Liberating Countries:
	- When the liberating country war goal is used successfully on a country, an event will appear that gives the country an infamy penalty for liberating the country
- Personal Union:
	- Countries can turn a newly released subject into a personal union, provided the country is a monarchy and that the action is done within 14 days of the subject being released. This is done by using the Personal Union diplomatic action on the newly released subject
- Unincorporate States:
	- To unincorporate an incorporated state, a country must use the Unincorporate State decree on the state that they want to unincorporate. This will enable an Unincorporate State decision to trigger an event, where the country will be given a final choice on whether to unincorporate the state or not

# Production Methods
- Production methods that use the transportation good are hidden until the transportation good is available in the state

# Scripted Buttons
- The African Colonial Administrations can no longer be formed by the AI
- Population the Americas:
	- If the country has either the state religion or state atheism law, the targetted country must be of the same religion as the country using the scripted button
	- If the country has the freedom of conscience law, the targetted country must be of the same religious group as the country using the scripted button
	- If the country has the total separation law, there are no religion requirements for the targetted country
	- Total population of the targetted country must be at least 3,000,000 or higher
	- The targetted country must have at least one incorporated state that is in Europe
	- The cost for using the scripted button has been changed to the following:
		- -25% influence
		- -10% authority
		- 0.025% of GDP

# Technology Spread
- The technology spread from excess innovation has been significantly reduced from 0.2 (20%) to 0.02 (2%), to reduce the effect of spamming universities for unrealistic technological progression in low literacy rate countries
- This has been replaced with a set of technology spread modifiers for each technology tree, that are applied to a country once it has researched all of the technologies in an era in each technology tree
- These modifiers are as follows:
	- Era 1 Complate: 10% technology spread bonus
	- Era 2 Complete: 20% technology spread bonus (total = 30% technology spread bonus)
	- Era 3 Complete: 30% technology spread bonus (total = 60% technology spread bonus)
	- Era 4 Complete: 40% technology spread bonus (total = 100% technology spread bonus)
